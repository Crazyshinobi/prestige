Prestige Group

Prestige Group is one of the leading real estate developers in India, with a strong presence in the southern part of the country, particularly in cities like Bangalore, Chennai, Hyderabad, Kochi, and Mysore. Founded in 1986 by Razack Sattar, the group has established itself as a trusted name in the real estate industry, known for its commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

Over the years, the Prestige Group has diversified its portfolio to include residential, commercial, retail, hospitality, and leisure developments. Some of their key projects include residential apartments, villas, luxury homes, IT parks, SEZs (Special Economic Zones), shopping malls, hotels, and resorts.

The group has earned numerous awards and accolades for its projects, including the CNBC Awaaz Real Estate Award, Asia Pacific Property Award, and the FIABCI Prix d'Excellence Award, among others.

Prestige Group is renowned for its focus on sustainability and green initiatives in its developments. Many of its projects are designed to meet international standards for energy efficiency and environmental sustainability.

Overall, the Prestige Group is recognized for its commitment to excellence, integrity, and delivering world-class real estate projects that cater to the evolving needs of its customers.
